The following data is to show the statistics of Gonorrhea. The data is shown to represent the years of 1941-2016. This is an 75 year period. As you can see, the data shown is out of a population of 100,000 people. The rates of this disease is neither linear or exponential. It begans to grow and then have sudden decreases. In this graph the highest number of the 100,000 people that obtained gonorrhea was 500. The year of 1977 was when there was the most amount of people with gonorrhea out of this population.

The table here shows a number of things. It shows not only how many people have this disease(gonorrhea) out of 100,000 but it also show's the people age.
